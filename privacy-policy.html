Privacy Policy

At Slimify, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you access and use our website and services, including our image and video compression tools. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Slimify, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Email addresses or other data you voluntarily provide, such as subscribing to newsletters.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, device information, and usage patterns collected automatically to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and optimize your uploaded files.
  • Analyze website usage to improve our tools.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Slimify uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that remember preferences during a session.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymized data to understand website usage and improve our services.

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function properly without them.

4. File Handling and Security

We take the security of your files seriously:

  • Files are processed temporarily and not stored permanently unless specified.
  • Encryption is used during transmission to prevent unauthorized access.
  • We retain minimal data for operational purposes and delete files automatically after processing.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system can guarantee 100% security.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to enhance functionality, including:

  • Analytics and advertising platforms that collect anonymized data.
  • Integrations for email or form submissions.

These providers follow strict privacy standards. Uploaded files are not shared with third parties for marketing.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have rights regarding your personal data:

  • Request access to personal information and corrections if needed.
  • Opt out of marketing communications anytime.
  • Manage cookies in your browser settings.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically as updates may occur.
